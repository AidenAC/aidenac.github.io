About Me

Strange arpeggios, punchy drums, a deep bass, and a meaningful melody are some of the elements the modern siren uses to draw in its listeners.

Dating back to the time of the greek gods, the siren has gone from luring sailors to captivating dance floors with a sound influenced by house from Chicago, techno from Detroit, and many of the greatest melody writers of our time.
